Our Services

At Khayaban Rehab Center, we offer a wide range of comprehensive services tailored to meet the unique needs of our patients. Explore our offerings below.

Detoxification and Medical Supervision: Safe, supervised detox to manage withdrawal symptoms.
Individual and Group Therapy: Evidence-based therapies like CBT, DBT, and peer support groups for mental and substance use disorders.
Family Counseling: Therapy sessions for families to build understanding and support networks.
Dual Diagnosis Treatment: Specialized care for patients with both substance use and mental health disorders.
Relapse Prevention: Training in coping skills and strategies to prevent relapse.
Holistic Therapies: Complementary therapies like mindfulness, yoga, art therapy, and recreational activities for a balanced approach to recovery.
Khayaban Rehab Center offers a range of comfortable accommodations designed to create a safe and supportive environment:
  • Individual Rooms: Private rooms with essential amenities for tranquility and recovery.
  • Shared Rooms: Options for two patients per room, fostering camaraderie and support.
  • Dedicated Wards: Group living spaces encouraging social interaction and community.
Each accommodation is thoughtfully designed to promote healing and comfort during recovery.
At Khayaban Rehab Center, we provide:
  • Medications: Tailored, up-to-date medicines administered safely for effective recovery.
  • Nutritious Meals: Balanced, hygienic diets to enhance physical and mental well-being.
Our holistic care approach ensures healthier lifestyles and supports patients on their journey to recovery.

Our Treatments

At Khayaban Rehab Center, we offer specialized treatment programs for various substance use and mental health disorders. Explore our treatments below.

We offer specialized treatment for a variety of addictions, including:
  • Heroin
  • Cannabis and Hashish
  • Amphetamines
  • Tranquilizers
  • Crystal Meth
  • Alcohol

We provide evidence-based therapies for a wide range of mental health conditions, including:
  • Depression and Anxiety
  • Bipolar Disorder

  • Obsessive-Compulsive Disorder (OCD)
  • Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD)
  • Eating Disorders
  • Personality Disorders

  • Borderline Personality Disorder
Our team provides comprehensive care and treatment plans customized for each individual.

Psychology Internship Program

At Khayaban Rehab Center, we are dedicated to fostering the next generation of mental health professionals through a structured internship program for undergraduate and graduate students. This program offers hands-on experience, where interns work directly with patients under expert supervision, allowing them to apply theoretical knowledge in real-world scenarios. By engaging with diverse cases, interns enhance their clinical skills, deepen their understanding of addiction and mental health treatment, and gain critical insights into patient care. Our commitment to excellence ensures that each intern leaves with the confidence and practical skills needed for a successful career in psychological and rehabilitative services.
